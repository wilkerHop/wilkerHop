PHASE 1: RESEARCH & CONTEXT

WRITING TECHNICAL POCS IN 2025

The era of dry, documentation-style articles is over. Modern technical writing treats a Proof of Concept (PoC) like a product launch: it needs a narrative, visual evidence, and immediate value. This guide deconstructs how to write articles that developers actually read.

1 THE SHIFT TO "SHOW, DON'T JUST TELL"

Readers ignore walls of text. The trend is Explorable Explanations. Use diagrams, gifs of the terminal, or interactive code blocks. Your PoC isn't just code; it's a solution to a problem.

2 THE "BUILD IN PUBLIC" VIBE

Perfection is suspicious. Modern articles embrace the "gotchas," the failed attempts, and the pivot. Authenticity builds trust. Frame your PoC as a journey of discovery.
