THE VANGUARD MANIFESTO

1. IDEOLOGICAL SUBVERSION: The narrative text is a site for political discourse. A critique must link fictional mechanics to real-world power structures.

3. INSTITUTIONAL CRITIQUE: Analyze the product as the result of industrial forces—production committees, animator overwork, market demands.
